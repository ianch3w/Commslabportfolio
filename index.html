Say Cheese!

Yes, that's me. I'm Ian, an amateur artist, dancer, and filmmaker, trying desperately to balance all these hobbies as a student at New York University Abu Dhabi. A big fan of comics, I started drawing from a young age inspired by artists like Patrick Brown and masterpices like Into the Spiderverse. Ever since, I haven't been able to stop doodling, filling out notebooks and eventually moving onto more unconventional canvases like shoes. My work is a reflection of my personality, and I love infusing things I'm passionate about into my projects, such as making a mockumentary about basketball or an interactive comic drawn in the style of Calvin and Hobbes.

My other hobbies include traveling and playing basketball, though I'm not very good at the second one. I'm pretty new to this coding thing but I hope you enjoy scrolling through my website! That's enough about me for now. If you like what you see, don't hesitate to reach out.
